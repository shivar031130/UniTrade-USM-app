Community Guidelines

Our goal is to create a safe and trusted community. We expect all users to adhere to these guidelines.

1. Be Respectful

Treat every user with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying are strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate account ban.

2. Post Honest Listings

Provide accurate descriptions and clear photos of your items. Be transparent about any defects or issues. Misleading listings erode trust and are subject to removal.

3. Communicate Clearly

Respond to messages promptly and politely. Clear communication is key to a smooth and successful transaction for both parties.
