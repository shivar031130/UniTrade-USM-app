Meet in Public

Always arrange to meet in a well-lit, public location on campus, like the library lobby or student union.

Inspect Before Paying

Thoroughly check the item to ensure it matches the description and is in the expected condition before you hand over any money.

Trust Your Gut

If a deal seems too good to be true, or if a user's behavior seems suspicious, it's okay to walk away. Your safety is the priority.

Use Secure Chat

Keep all communication within the UniTrade chat system. Avoid sharing personal contact information like your phone number or social media.
